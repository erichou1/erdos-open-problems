# Claim–Evidence Ledger

Status vocabulary:

- **Established for artifact**: directly checkable in released code/proof/logs.
- **Supported**: primary paper plus meaningful artifact, controlled review, or independent evidence.
- **Author claim**: reported by system authors but not independently reproduced here.
- **Inference**: architectural conclusion drawn from cited evidence.
- **Rejected/obsolete**: wording contradicted or superseded by stronger/current evidence.
