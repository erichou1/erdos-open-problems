## Source-quality policy applied

Peer-reviewed papers and controlled benchmark artifacts were preferred where available. Recent preprints were treated as author claims unless accompanied by runnable code, released proofs, independent audit, or expert review. Company pages were used for narrow public facts, not undisclosed architecture inference. When a public result artifact existed without generation code, the proof and the autonomy/reproducibility claim were graded separately.
