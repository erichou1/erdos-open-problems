\noindent\textbf{Problem Statement:}

Let $d_n=p_{n+1}-p_n$, where $p_n$ denotes the $n$th prime. Is it true that\[\frac{\max_{n<x}d_{n}d_{n-1}}{(\max_{n<x}d_n)^2}\to 0\]as $x\to \infty$?
