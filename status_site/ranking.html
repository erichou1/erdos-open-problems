How this ranking should be read

SHARED ALLOCATION

The same order used by every coordinated worker

The base acquisition score is adjusted by bounded, auditable literature signals. Scores are search preferences—not solution probabilities or measures of mathematical difficulty.

RESEARCH PROGRESS

Highest observable milestone—not percent solved

The progress bar advances when the pipeline fixes the interpretation, explores approaches, proposes a concrete claim, checks evidence, or verifies formal work.

PRIZE POLICY

All eligible unpaid problems come first

Paid targets are deferred until eligible unpaid problems are exhausted. Prize size is never treated as a calibrated estimate of difficulty.

LITERATURE COVERAGE

Coverage and outages remain visible

Local and live literature can change selection preference within a prize tier. Missing providers are recorded neutrally rather than counted as negative mathematical evidence.
