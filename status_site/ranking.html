How this ranking should be read

SOLVABILITY INDEX

Fit with this pipeline—not mathematical difficulty

The index favors problems with an available Lean route, a finite exact-computation route, and a clear statement. A score of 40 does not mean a 40% chance of solution.

RESEARCH PROGRESS

Highest observable milestone—not percent solved

The progress bar advances when the pipeline fixes the interpretation, explores approaches, proposes a concrete claim, checks evidence, or verifies formal work.
