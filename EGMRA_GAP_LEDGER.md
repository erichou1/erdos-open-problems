# EGMRA Gap Ledger

**Audit date:** 2026-07-14  
**Severity scale:** Critical = can create a false proof or defeats the primary mission; High = blocks a required end-to-end capability; Medium = serious reliability/coverage limitation; Low = bounded operability or observability issue.  
**Likelihood:** likelihood of encountering the gap in realistic use, not likelihood that the diagnosis is correct.

## Blocker classification

- **External credentials/services:** only U-09 is directly blocked by a valid Aristotle credential. Live Semantic Scholar throttling is an external condition, but U-10 is still an internal error-reporting defect.
- **Missing specification:** none prevented this audit. Some mathematical review policies need product decisions, but the normative documents are sufficient to identify the gaps.
- **Fundamental architectural work:** U-01 through U-08 and U-14 require significant design/implementation beyond safe audit remediation.
- **Safe next fixes:** U-10, U-11, U-12, U-13, U-19, U-20, U-21, and U-24 can be delivered incrementally with behavior-level regressions.
